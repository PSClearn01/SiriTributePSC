Here's a time line of Orlando Salido's life:

  • 1980 - Orlando Salido was born on November 16, 1980 in Ciudad Obregon, Sonora, Mexico.
  • 1996 - Salido made his professional debut at the age of 15.
  • 2006 - Salido won his first world title, the IBF featherweight title, by defeating Robert Guerrero.
  • 2014 - Salido defeated Vasyl Lomachenko, who was 1-0 at the time, by split decision to retain his WBO featherweight title.
  • 2017 - Salido fought to a draw against Miguel Roman in his last fight before retiring.
